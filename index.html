Woodmont Psychiatric Group has served the Washington area for over five decades. We pride ourselves in providing the highest quality mental health care possible. We are committed to providing an environment that is compassionate and reliable. We dedicate our time, skills, and energy to this objective. Our goal is to work with our patients in a cooperative effort, so that they will enjoy optimal mental health.

We are a general psychiatric practice with doctors who have specialized expertise in a number of areas, in order to provide a variety of psychiatric services to adults, couples/families, children and adolescents. These services include:

  • Comprehensive Psychiatric Evaluations
  • Individual Psychotherapy
  • Marital / Couples / Family Therapy
  • Psychopharmacological Evaluations and Medication Monitoring
  • Specialized Groups
  • Forensic Assessments
