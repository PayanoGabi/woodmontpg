Woodmont Psychiatric Group has served the Washington area for over five decades. We pride ourselves in providing the highest quality mental health care possible. We are committed to providing an environment that is compassionate and reliable. We dedicate our time, skills, and energy to this objective. Our goal is to work with our patients in a cooperative effort, so that they will enjoy optimal mental health.

We are a general psychiatric practice with doctors who have specialized expertise in a number of areas, in order to provide a variety of psychiatric services to adults, couples/families, children and adolescents. These services include:

  • Comprehensive Psychiatric Evaluations
  • Individual Psychotherapy
  • Marital / Couples / Family Therapy
  • Psychopharmacological Evaluations and Medication Monitoring
  • Specialized Groups
  • Forensic Assessments

Martin J. Book, D.O.

Dr. Book has been a practicing psychiatrist since 1979.

Dr. Book is Board Certified by:

  • The American Board of Psychiatry and Neurology
  • The American Osteopathic Board of Neurology and Psychiatry

He has a special interest in Psychopharmacology and Psychotherapy, with a focus on treating anxiety disorders, depressive disorders, and attention deficit / hyperactivity disorder. He has served as a principal investigator for a number of industry-sponsored clinical trials.

After graduating from The University of Maryland at College Park, he earned his medical degree from the Des Moines University, College of Osteopathic Medicine and Surgery, Des Moines, Iowa. He completed his Residency in Psychiatry at the Karl Menninger School of Psychiatry, Topeka, Kansas. Following that, he received an appointment to the Menninger Clinic Faculty until joining Woodmont Psychiatric Group in 1986.

Dr. Book is a Fellow of the American Psychiatric Association (F.A.P.A.). He is also a member of several local and national medical organizations, including:

  • The American Psychiatric Association
  • The Washington Psychiatric Society
  • The Maryland State Medical Society
  • The Montgomery County Medical Society

Dr. Book has been consistently listed in the Washingtonian magazine as one of the area's "TOP DOCTORS" in Psychiatry.

Dr. Book is listed as a "TOP DOCTOR" in the Washington Consumers' Checkbook. He has also received the Patients' Choice award, which is awarded based upon a survey of over 100,000 patients, nationwide. Less than 6% of doctors receive this award.
